Colorstrap Striped Progress Bars

Represent progress in 140 named HTML colors, striped, of course. These classes work with Bootstrap progress bars.

Example

41% Complete FireBrick
<div class="progress">
   <div class="progress-bar colorstrap-striped progress-bar-firebrick" role="progressbar" aria-valuenow="41" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 41%">
      41% Complete FireBrick
   </div>
</div>
